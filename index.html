TechFun4All

I am 14 years old, and intestered in all things Raspberry Pi, coding, electronics and making. I have a rare bone condition, called MHE - Multiple Herediary Exostoses, or HME, which means I get chronic pain and fatigue. I want to help others, especially young people, like me, to get into creatively using technology, to free their mental or physical boundaries from disability.

This is my website, where I blog about events, etc, document my projects, and help others get into tech! I've hand coded it from scratch, using Github Pages, and although it has taken a while, it's looking pretty good now, I think! I'm working on documenting some projects, and getting the blog working, so I'll update the site when it's ready, but please feel free to contact me in the meantime (see my contact page) :)
